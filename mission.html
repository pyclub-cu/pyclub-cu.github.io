Our mission

PyClub provides interactive online coding classes, taught by real-world ocean scientists

Our team's missions:

1. Introduce high school students to oceanography, as well as the tools to analyze data scientifically, in an engaging, positive and creative way.

2. Provide high school students with real examples of who scientists are, what we do, and why we care.

3. Provide high-quality mentorship with students, made possible by our low student-teacher ratios. We believe mentorships to be of utmost importance in breaking barriers to STEM careers
